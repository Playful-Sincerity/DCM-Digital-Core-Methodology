**Why:** Wisdom's framing crystallized over the session, and several reframes need to persist for future writing:

- **IVNA is not the fullest illustration.** It is one example from the research arm, alongside HHA from the operations arm and others. The paper's value is the breadth, not the depth in any one case.
- **GVR is not the most important pattern.** It is one organ. The configuration layer + document substrate is what makes the methodology work.
- **Memory is not the right frame; documents is.** *Storage is cheap, context is sacred.* The chronicle, MEMORY.md, per-topic memory files, `ideas/`, `research/`, `knowledge/sources/`, `remote-entries/` are documents the AI writes and both parties read. Stop calling it memory.
- **The 1960s lineage was surfaced by PSDC's prior-art research, not imported by Wisdom.** Honest credit matters: the paper now distinguishes Wisdom's discoveries from PSDC-surfaced syntheses, and points at the implication (the architecture may be discoverable from the structure of the problem rather than inherited from theory).
- **The bigger category the paper sits inside is *digital core software*.** Custom software with an agentic editor inside it that the user owns and keeps editing — sold via consulting rather than SaaS subscription. HHA + CRB (Customer Relationship Builder) are one early instance. Hedged with "people have been arriving at it from different angles for a little while now" — early instantiation, not sole invention.

**Means:** A few structural patterns to honor in future writing on this paper and adjacent artifacts:

- *PSDC found this*, not *I imported this* — when an AI-surfaced connection is being reported, name the AI as the discoverer.
- *Examples across domains*, not *one case study* — Wisdom's instinct is to honor breadth deliberately because the methodology's value is its domain-generality.
- *Modest paradigm coining* — when introducing new categories like "digital core software," frame as integrator-of-things-already-happening rather than sole-invention. Hedge with explicit acknowledgment of parallel work.
- *Concrete examples land claims* — the paper now has a real chronicle example (an edit in one conversation broke parallel work, caught instantly by the chronicle) instead of an abstract assertion that the chronicle helps. That kind of grounding makes the document-layer claim land.
