# Research

Supporting research artifacts for the Digital Core Methodology paper.

## Contents

### `paper-decision-agents/`
Raw outputs from the `/think-deep` session on 2026-04-18 that chose the paper's direction. Five agents ran:

- **`research-web.md`** — web research on what lands well at AI practitioner reading clubs
- **`play-thread-follower.md`** — creative exploration (threads worth following further)
- **`play-paradox-holder.md`** — creative exploration (tensions to hold)
- **`play-pattern-spotter.md`** — creative exploration (cross-domain patterns)
- **`structure.md`** — analytical synthesis with decision landscape and confidence scores

These are the primary data behind the paper-direction decision described in `chronicle/2026-04-18.md`.

## Convention

Research artifacts here are meant to be the paper's *showing-the-work*: anyone reading the paper should be able to trace any claim back to the agent output or research file that produced it. This follows the `raw-data-preservation` rule — storage is cheap, re-deriving research is expensive.
